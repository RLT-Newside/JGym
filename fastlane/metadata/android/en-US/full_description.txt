JGym is a minimalist, offline-first gym workout tracker.

Features:
• Log exercises, sets, reps, and weight during your session
• Automatic personal record (PR) detection and badges
• Session history with a calendar overview
• Exercise library with muscle group filters and body map
• Built-in workout plans wiki
• Dark theme, no ads, no accounts, no tracking
• All data stored locally on your device

No internet connection required. No sign-up. No data leaves your phone.
